Ch.7 LinearEquations in Two Unknowns

7.1 Linear Equations in Two Unknowns and Their Graphs

  1. （not a solution）

Determine whether the following pairs are solutions of the equation (eg. 2x+y=42x+y=42x+y=4 )
e.g. (4,0)

Solution:

Sub. (4,0) into the equation
L.H.S. = 2(4)+0
          = 8
          ≠ R.H.S.
∵ L.H.S.  ≠ R.H.S.
∴ (4,0) is not a solution of the equation.
  1. (is a solution)

Determine whether the following pairs are solutions of the equation (eg. 2x+y=42x+y=42x+y=4 )
e.g. (3,-2)

Solution:

Sub. (3,-2) into the equation.
L.H.S. =2(3)+(-2)
          =6-2
          =4
          =R.H.S.
∵ L.H.S.  = R.H.S.
∴ (3,-2) is a solution of the equation.
