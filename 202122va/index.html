爱奇艺

YouTube

功课

  1. Draw an object in 3-5 different perspectives (you may draw, cut, copy and paste the object). Give different values to the objects with drawing pencils. The attached work is an example.

  2. Search information of Pablo Picasso and Cubism. (You don't need to upload the information but need to share in the next lesson)

Step

  1. 在一张白纸素描一样物品
  2. 剪出素描的物品
  3. 用铅笔（建议使用 2B 或以上的铅笔）于正反面涂满碳粉
  4. 把剪出的纸张放在 Visual Dairy 上，用纸巾把碳粉揉到附近
  5. 重复几遍
  6. 用铅笔涂抹重复部分
